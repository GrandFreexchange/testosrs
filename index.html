Trend Confidence

Confidence is a rating based on the item's 24-hour trade volume. High volume suggests the price trend is more reliable.

  • High: >50k volume or >5x buy limit.
  • Medium: >10k volume or >1x buy limit.
  • Low: All other cases.

Profit Calculator

Profit Calculation

Profit is calculated using the following formula:

(Sell Price - Buy Price) * Qty - GE Tax

The Grand Exchange (GE) tax is 2% of the total sale price, but is reduced to 1% for certain items (like runes, arrows, and bonds) or for sales over 250m GP.
